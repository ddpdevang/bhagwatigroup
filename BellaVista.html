Bay Bliss offers an environment that is perfectly designed for a luxurious and peaceful living. The location and beauty of luxuriously designed spaces are here to make your everyday full of conveniences and exceptional experience. We have specially designed 2 & 3 BHK flats that you can choose from. No matter what flat size you choose, when you enter you will discover that everything is perfectly designed to win your appreciation. Perfect surroundings and accessibility will ensure that your life will be full with memorable and happy moments.

  • Clear CIDCO transfer plot.
  • G+13 storeyed residential cum commercial complex.
  • Exclusive 2 BHK and 3 BHK flats and shops.
  • Earthquake resistant RCC design.
  • Designer entrance lobby.
  • 2 hi-speed elevators in each wing.
  • Generator backup for lobby, lift, stairs & pumps.
  • Spacious and designer floor lobbies.
  • Open and cover parking on ground and 1st floor.
  • Jogging track, clubhouse and fitness centre.
  • Indoor sports with pool table, carrom and chess.
  • Senior citizens corner.
  • Party lawn with landscaped garden.
  • Separate children’s play area.
  • Swimming pool with kid’s pool.

Flooring 1. Vitrified flooring in all rooms.
2. Anti-skid tiles in all terrace flooring.

Kitchen 1. Granite kitchen platform with service platform.
2. Branded stainless steel sink.
3. Glazed wall tiles up to beam level.

Doors and Windows 1. Wooden laminated flush door in every room.
2. Good quality fixture and fittings.
3. Anodized coated heavy section aluminium sliding on granite sills with tinted glass.

Bath and WC 1. Designer bathroom with branded sanitary ware.
2. Concealed plumbing with premium quality CP fittings.
3. Spartex/glazed tiles in flooring & glazed wall tiles up to beam height.
4. Waterproof ERP bathroom doors.
5. Louvered glass window in anodized aluminium frame.

Wall and Paints 1. Internal walls with putty finish.
2. Paints of premium quality on interior walls and acrylic paints on outer walls.

Electrification 1. Concealed copper wiring with adequate points and modular switches.
2. 1/3 phase electricity connection (as per flat size) in every flat with circuit breaker ELCB.

Security Video door security system in each flat with CCTV cameras at every gate.
