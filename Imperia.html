Ulwe is one of the prime property destinations in Navi Mumbai that is well connected via rail and road, and is also rich in natural vegetation which makes it a perfect place to lead a world class lifestyle.

  • Road access from Mumbai-Pune Expressway and NH4B.
  • Close to proposed Mumbai –Sewri road, through Nhava Sheva Sea Link.
  • Only 5 Kms from CBD Belapur Railway Station and adjacent to proposed Bamandongri Railway Station.
  • Close proximity to service industries like IT, SEZ and services related to airports.
  • Proximity to Nhava Sheva Docks (JNPT) to south.
  • Interconnected to Proposed rail link between Belapur & Uran.
  • Just 7 minutes from proposed Navi Mumbai international airport.
  • Schools, colleges, hospitals, restaurants etc. close by.
