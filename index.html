About Us

Since its inception in 2002, Bhagwati Group has earned its reputation as a leading Real Estate Development Firm in and around Navi Mumbai. We pride ourselves on developing smart, high-tech buildings, always with a sustainable footprint. Working with a strong team of industry professionals, we have a proven track record in unlocking value in sites and translating client visions into reality.


Whether you’re looking to invest in new build schemes or are an existing landowner with a joint venture idea, Bhagwati Group has the collective skills and experience to translate your ideas.
